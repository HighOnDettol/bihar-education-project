The Bihar Education Project

Understanding the Paradox: A Micro-Level Analysis of Fund Utilization Failure in Government Schools

Our Motive & Project Overview

Despite significant government expenditure on education in Bihar, the quality of schooling remains a persistent concern. Our research aims to unravel this "Bihar Education Paradox" by conducting a micro-level analysis of how educational funds are utilized at the grassroots level in government schools.

We believe that understanding the specific points of fund utilization failure, systemic governance issues, and their direct impact on educational outcomes is crucial for developing evidence-based policy interventions. This project employs a mixed-methods approach, combining quantitative data analysis with qualitative insights from stakeholders like yourself.

Your valuable insights as a teacher are central to this study. By sharing your experiences and perceptions through this response sheet, you contribute directly to a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities for reform in Bihar's education sector.
